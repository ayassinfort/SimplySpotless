Website Overview

This software is designed to assist user to schedule their desired cleaning service.

The Simply Spotless website allows users to view available cleaning services and request professional cleaning using a simple online form. The site is structured as follows:

  • Header: Contains the company name and slogan.
  • Navigation Bar: Links to Services and Request Form.
  • Services Section: Detailed list of services offered.
  • Request Form: For users to submit cleaning requests.

Using the Services Page

The Services section highlights the various cleaning services offered by Simply Spotless. Each service is displayed in a card layout for quick readability.

Hovering over a service card will highlight it, giving the user a visual cue.

Filling Out the Request Form

To request a service, navigate to the bottom of the website or click "Request Service" from the navigation bar. Fill in the required fields:

  • Your Name
  • Your Email
  • Your Phone Number
  • Service Details

Click the Submit Request button to send your form. You will receive a confirmation email shortly after submission.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form?

Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your service request and schedule a time.

Can I request multiple services at once?

Yes. You can detail all your cleaning needs in the message box of the request form.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. As long as we have access to the premises, our team can carry out the service.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Form not submitting

Ensure all required fields are filled. Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, email support@simplyspotless.com.

Issue: I didn't receive a confirmation email

Check your spam folder. If it's not there, contact our support team.

Issue: Page not loading correctly

Try clearing your browser cache or opening the site in a different browser.
