Database Overview

The database structure supports core operations such as client and employee management, service scheduling, supply tracking, and financial documentation.

Clients

Each client is stored in the Clients table with their contact information. They are linked to one or more properties in the Properties table.

  • Key fields: ClientID, FirstName, LastName, Email
  • See how Clients relate to Properties

Employees

The Employees table contains all personnel information including address, contact, and pay rate.

  • Used for assigning cleaning jobs via the AssignedEmployees table
  • Track time in EmployeeTimesheet

Scheduled Services

The ScheduledServices table manages cleaning appointments including timing, type, and linked property and employee data.

  • Linked with Services for service type and price
  • Check Supplies Needed for each scheduled service

Supplies

The Supplies and SuppliesNeeded tables track what materials are required per job and current inventory levels.

  • SuppliesNeeded links to a ScheduledService to specify required materials
  • SuppliesPurchaseHistory logs all supply purchases

Employee Timesheets

The EmployeeTimesheet table records work hours, breaks, and calculated totals for payroll purposes.

  • Entries are linked to individual EmployeeIDs
  • Back to Employee Details

Invoices

The Invoices table stores billing data linked to a Client and a specific ScheduledService.

  • Includes status, total, and due amount
  • Helps track payments and outstanding balances
